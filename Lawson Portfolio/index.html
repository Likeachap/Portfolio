Audiovisuals

BandGame2

Neuroscience-driven VR rhythm game for the Oculus Rift to train polyrhythmic coordination between hands. Developed with Unity's VR toolkit and sprites made in Aseprite. Shown and playtested in CCRMA!

Naturalistic Sequencing

Using custom 2D colliders in ChuGL, shooting stars pluck user drawn constellations, generating familiarly natural rhythms.

Bloom, particle effects, and improved collision detection disabled due to performance. I'm working on getting a new laptop!

The Wind => Effects

A digital sculpture and real-time audio visualizer mapping the frequency spectrum and amplitudes of ChucK-generated wind and chime sounds onto grass blade x/z offsets and rain parameters. 

A study on artfully reversing natural systems to achieve emulation; using noise to drive movement where such relationship is generally reversed.

Waves: A soundtoy modulating wave-equation constants and synthesis parameters to organically flex and breathe.

Thematically, I'm inspired by the digitalization of Western aesthetics. Along California highways you'll see radio towers disguised as pine trees, and near Seattle you'll see water silos painted as evergreen forests. Tech bros wear Arcteryx as if their desktop is going to start raining. How we contain feelings of being in nature into software motivates most of my work; I want to drive this Western simulacra past discernability.
